## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

BuildFlow is a comprehensive cloud-based construction and project management platform designed to help government agencies and organizations efficiently manage projects, vendors, assets, and procurement. This proposal outlines a 4-week pilot program to demonstrate the platform's capabilities for managing Ministry of Interior projects and vendor relationships.

## PILOT SUCCESS CRITERIA

At the end of the 4-week pilot, we will measure success based on:

1. **Platform Adoption** - Number of active users and login frequency
2. **Projects Created** - At least 3 projects fully set up and tracked
3. **Vendor Onboarding** - Minimum 10 vendors registered and profiled
4. **User Satisfaction** - Survey score of 4.0/5.0 or higher
5. **Process Improvement** - Documented efficiency gains in project management
